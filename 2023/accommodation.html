Accommodation Options

Conference attendees are encouraged to book their stay at one of the three hotels listed on this page. Alternatively, you may select any hotel located in or near the Melbourne CBD. Please note that the conference venue is situated within the Melbourne CBD, and is accessible via Melbourne's Free Tram Zone.

For more information about Melbourne's Free Tram Zone, please refer to Free-Tram-Zone-Map.


Option 1: Pullman Melbourne on Swanston

Please see the below code to quote when booking by contacting our group's reservation team.


Code: VICUNI2023


Booking Details:

Code will be for booking between 22nd October 2023 to 27th October 2023 and valid for Pullman Melbourne on Swanston only.

Quoting the above will provide the guest with a discount of 10% off the Best Available Rate and a fully flexible rate, which means guests can cancel the booking up to 24 hours before arrival.

Please note this booking will be subject to availability.

When guests wish to book, they must contact our group reservation team at H3028-re7@accor.com and quote the above code.

For more information about the hotel, please refer to their official website PULLMAN.

Option 2: The Jazz Corner Hotel Melbourne

The Jazz Corner Hotel, a melody of comfort and culture in Melbourne's vibrant CBD. Perfectly tuned for international conference attendees, this jazz-inspired hotel offers not only a convenient location—a short stroll or quick tram ride from the conference venue—but also an inviting gateway to explore the city's famed music scene and culinary delights. With its thoughtful design and premier amenities, The Jazz Corner Hotel promises guests a harmonious blend of convenience and exploration in the heart of Melbourne.

For more information about the hotel, please refer to their official website JAZZ CORNER.

Option 3: Mantra on Little Bourke Melbourne

Mantra on Little Bourke is the perfect base to discover all the excitement of the city of Melbourne. Centrally located in Melbourne's CBD, we're close to famous eat streets, shopping hubs and cultural experiences such as Melbourne Art Gallery, Melbourne Museum and Federation Square.

For more information about the hotel, please refer to their official website MANTRA.
