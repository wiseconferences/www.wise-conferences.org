Registration

Dear paper authors, who didn't come to the conference or/and didn't receive the payment receipt. Send please a request to rashid.zalyalov@gmail.com if you need the receipt.

The registration procedure is common for WISE2017 and HIS2017. Attendees of these (any one or both) conferences have to be registered once.

At least one author of each accepted paper must fill out the registration form by Aug 30, 2017, in order for the paper to be included in the conference proceedings. Non-author attendees and additional authors of accepted papers have to be registered to get an Invitation from Organization Committee. The registration fee is the US $600 ($300 for students) before Aug 30, 2017, after will be the US $650. This includes a conference kit, coffee breaks, and a banquet dinner. Each paper to be published must accompany with a full registration US$600 (US$650).
