From Melbourne Airport (Tullamarine) to CBD:
• Skybus: runs between the Melbourne Airport (Tullamarine) and Southern Cross Train Station (CBD) every 10 min, end-to-end bus service with no stops in between, around 20 min's trip, $18 per Adult one way. There are also family and group discount, which can be found at skybus website.
• Taxi: from Melbourne Airport (Tullamarine) to CBD, around 20 min's trip, $67 variable depending traffic condition. There are plenty of taxis at the airport. To call a taxi back to the airport, you may call 13CABS (stands for 132227) to make a booking or go to 13CABS website to place a booking.


From Southern Cross Station to Conference Venue:
• Tram: take tram 75 towards Flinders Street Station, get off at Stop Flinders Street Station (on Flinders street near Elizabeth street). Around 10 min. Free cost.
• Walk: 16 min said by Google.




From Southern Cross Station to the hotels:
• Rendezvous Hotel Melbourne: the same as to Conference Venue
• Vibe Savoy: very near to South Cross Station.
• Travelodge Southbank: take tram 75 and get off at Flinders Street Station, walk across yarra river via a bridge and will find it at South Bank.
• Quest on William: 10 min walk towards william street from Southern Cross Station.


From the hotels to the conference venue:
• Vibe Savoy: take tram 75 towards Flinders Street Station, get off at Stop Flinders Street Station (on Flinders street near Elizabeth street).
• Others: walk.
