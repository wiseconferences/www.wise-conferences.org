  Information for authors:

At least one author for each accepted paper must complete the registration by Aug 26, 2016 in order for the paper to be included in the conference proceedings. The author registration fee is US $700 or RMB ￥4650 for all authors. This includes a conference kit, coffee breaks, lunches, a banquet dinner, and access to all conference sessions. 

  Information for attendees

For non-author attendees and additional authors of accepted papers, the following registration options are available. All registrations include a conference kit, coffee breaks, lunches, a banquet dinner, and access to all conference sessions.

* The 5th International Conference on Health Information Science (HIS 2016) will be held in Nov.5-Nov.7 2016 at the same hotel. For more information about the HIS 2016, you can get them from the following website.

 http://www.his-conferences.org/2016/index.html

To attend both conferences, please pay the combined WISE2016&HIS2016 fees.
